Crisis Playbook (FAQ)

Someone is begging for a promotion?

Remind them that promotions are earned through war performance, activity, and donations. Let them know that repeatedly asking will delay their promotion. As an Elder or Co-leader, submit a strike log form with the user's information and include your name for reference. Be sure to add helpful notes!

Someone is attacking the wrong number in wars

On the first offense, issue a warning. After multiple strikes are recorded in the strike log, the member may be removed from the clan. You can review members’ strike history and records in the database tab.
