Crisis Playbook (FAQ)

Someone is begging for a promotion?

Remind them that promotions are earned through war performance, activity, and donations. Let them know that repeatedly asking will delay their promotion. As an Elder or Co-leader, submit a strike log form with the user's information and include your name for reference. Be sure to add helpful notes!

Someone is attacking the wrong number in wars

On the first offense, issue a warning. After multiple strikes are recorded in the strike log, the member may be removed from the clan. You can review members’ strike history and records in the database tab.

Coleader Promoting Elder

If you're a Coleader accepting an Elder Application, unlike the other rules this one is very strict and it's important that you put the user's username in the Discord Recruits. Also, you need to make sure the member is in our Discord. Once the Clan Leader accepts it, the member will be provided with assistance. View Application Forms Here

Coleader starting Polls

Polls are very easy to start; you can create them at any time for any reason (e.g., a new clan logo). Use /poll in the Leader Commands in Discord.

Coleader Changing Clan Rules

Coleaders are allowed to change the clan rules, but they must discuss it with other leaders before doing so. Changing rules without discussion will result in a warning.

What use is the Messages in the GLW?

These messages are very important, as they only get sent out if something major is happening. Always check messages in the app if there is an exclamation point next to "Messages" on the navigation bar. They are useful and will not be spammed.
