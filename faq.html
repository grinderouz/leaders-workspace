Crisis Playbook (FAQ)

How To install the GLW App ⬇️📲

Android:
1. Open this site in Chrome.
2. Tap the menu (⋮) button in the top-right.
3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4. Follow prompts—GLW will appear on your home screen!

iOS (iPhone/iPad):
1. Open this site in Safari.
2. Tap the Share (🔗) button.
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap Add—GLW will appear on your home screen!

PC (Windows, Mac, Linux):
1. Open this site in Chrome, Edge, or Brave.
2. Look for the install (➕ or a computer icon) button in the address bar—click it.
3. Or, open menu (⋮ or ≡) & choose "Install GLW".
4. Follow prompts to add the app to your desktop!

Someone is begging for a promotion? 🏆🙋‍♂️

Remind them that promotions are earned through war performance, activity, and donations. Let them know that repeatedly asking will delay their promotion. 🚫⏳ As an Elder or Co-leader, submit a strike log form with the user's information and include your name for reference. Be sure to add helpful notes! 📋📝

Someone is attacking the wrong number in wars ⚔️❌

On the first offense, issue a warning. ⚠️ After multiple strikes are recorded in the strike log, the member may be removed from the clan. 👋 You can review members’ strike history and records in the database tab. 📑

Coleader Promoting Elder 👑⬆️

If you're a Coleader accepting an Elder Application, unlike the other rules this one is very strict and it's important that you put the user's username in the Discord Recruits. 💻📝 Also, you need to make sure the member is in our Discord. Once the Clan Leader accepts it, the member will be provided with assistance. ✋🛡️ View Application Forms Here

Coleader starting Polls 📊🗳️

Polls are very easy to start; you can create them at any time for any reason (e.g., a new clan logo). 🎨 Use /poll in the Leader Commands in Discord. 💬

Coleader Changing Clan Rules 📝🔄

Coleaders are allowed to change the clan rules, but they must discuss it with other leaders before doing so. Changing rules without discussion will result in a warning. ⚠️🗣️

What use is the Messages in the GLW? 💬🔔

These messages are very important, as they only get sent out if something major is happening. ⚡ Always check messages in the app if there is an exclamation point next to "Messages" on the navigation bar. ❗ They are useful and will not be spammed. 👍

What is the Strike System? 🚨📋

Whether you choose to use it or not, we have it there. Our very own strike system where you can fill out a strike form. Choose to blacklist, warn, or whatever to a player in the clan. 🛑✍️ And always keep it there on record! However, this is optional—most Elders don't use it because they haven't discovered it or because it's useless maybe? 🤔
