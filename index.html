Rules & Ranks

BOTH ROLES

Strike Log Rules

When issuing a warning or strike, submit a Strike Log Form. This is for missed war attacks and related issues (not inactivity kicks). If the user is in Discord, you may give a strike log and ping warning—keep it fair for everyone. Blacklisting is available if needed.

LOA - Leave of Absence

If you go on vacation or will be inactive, post your expected return in the Discord LOA channel. Extended inactivity may result in removal, but a spot will be kept for you. This applies to Elders and Co-leaders only.

ELDER RULES

Set the Example
Elders must uphold all clan rules and model behavior for new members.

Welcome & Support
Welcome newcomers and answer questions in chat or Discord. Help maintain a positive clan atmosphere.

Maintain Activity
Stay active, donate regularly, and participate in wars, clan games, and raids.

CO-LEADER RULES

Minimum Town Hall
You must have at least Town Hall 11 on one of your accounts to become a Co-leader.

No Promoting Other Elders
Co-leaders can accept elder applications, but may NOT promote anyone to Co-leader or promote an Elder to Co-leader.

No Rogue Kicking
Do not kick any member without a documented reason (e.g., missed war attacks, toxicity). Kicking Elders must be discussed with the Leader first. Excessive kicking will be questioned.

Recruits Announcements
When promoting a member to Elder, post their name, tag, and Discord user in the Recruits channel. Failure to do so may result in a warning.

LEADER REQUIREMENTS

Elder Requirements
1000+ donations, full participation in consecutive wars, and at least 1 month in the clan.

Co-leader Requirements
Town Hall 11+, 3+ months in the clan, active participation.

Clan Mail
Use global messages only for important updates such as war starts, clan games goals, or raid weekend reminders. Avoid spamming.

Promotion Integrity
Never promote anyone to Elder or Co-leader just because they ask. Follow all established requirements.
